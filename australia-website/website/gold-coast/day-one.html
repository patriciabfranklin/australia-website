VISIT A THEME PARK

The theme parks on the Gold Coast are renowned throughout Australia as highly entertaining destinations, and they draw millions of visitors each and every year, and there are a variety of parks that each place an emphasis on a different form of entertainment. Choose from single park entries to parks such as Dreamworld, Sea World, Movie World, Wet n Wild and Whitewater World, or save money and get multiple visits by buying any one of the many great value theme park passes available, each of which provide different benefits to the pass holder. Whether you choose to visit one, two or all of the Gold Coast’s theme parks, you’ll end up having plenty to do that will more than fill your days.
