SKYPOINT CLIMB

The Q1 towers over Surfers Paradise as the world’s tallest residential building, and its Skypoint Observation Deck is the best place on the Gold Coast from which to gain spectacular views of the scenery that stretches from Brisbane in the north to Byron Bay in the south. You needn’t just stand on the observation deck however; the new Skypoint Climb experience allows you to go OUTSIDE the glass and experience an open-air adventure atop the building 270 meters above sea level. Harnessed to a purpose-built safety rail system, you’ll be escorted up the walkway and enjoy unparalleled views, expert commentary and more.
