GOLD COAST

The Gold Coast has long been Australia’s premier holiday destination for people of all persuasions – with one of the widest ranges of things to do of any region for visitors both local and international, the Gold Coast is a holiday playground full of activities, attractions, tours and more – both the man-made and the natural. Mention the Gold Coast to any Australian family and what almost certain will come to mind will be the dazzling array of Gold Coast beaches, nightclubs, theme parks, and more that mean there is truly something for everyone.
