SCENIC DAY TOUR OF THE GOLD COAST

The Gold Coast’s hinterland offers an impressive array of rolling hills, lush mountains and spectacular views all located within easy reach of Surfers Paradise and other main accommodation areas. Providing a refreshing, natural break from the glitz and glamour of the Gold Coast’s center, the hinterland is one of the region’s best-kept secrets, and a tour will take you to some of the most verdant, lush and visually striking displays of waterfalls, rainforests and amazing lookout points in South-East Queensland.
