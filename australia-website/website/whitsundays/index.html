WHITSUNDAYS

Located in the center of the coast of Queensland, the Whitsundays consist of 74 separate individual islands, the Whitsundays offers a plethora of marine and beach-based entertainment and leisure options, with each of the main islands in the group offering something slightly different and more unique to the rest of its family. Whitsunday Island is the largest in the chain and is home to numerous examples of pristine nature, but other popular destinations such as Daydream, Hamilton, and Long Islands are hugely popular with visitors as well.
