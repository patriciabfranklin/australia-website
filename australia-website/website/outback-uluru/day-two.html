KATA TJUTA

Head to the Kata Tjuta Duna Viewing area for sunrise, then experience the Walpa Gorge Walk before having a morning tea picnic at the Kata Tjuta Sunset Viewing area. See the amazing views in some of the Valley of the Winds Walks. Relax and unwind and head back to the Culture Center or the resort to have a rest, a swim or join an activity. In the afternoon make sure to head back to the Kata Tjuta Sunset Viewing Area for sunset.
