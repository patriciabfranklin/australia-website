ULURU

Visit Talinguru Nyakunytjaku for sunrise. Stay on and explore the Minymaku and Watiku walks. Later take a walk around Uluru on the Uluru Base Trail (3.5 hours). Later head to the Cultural Centre for lunch and to learn more about Anangu culture and see Anangu art. Next make sure to relax and unwind. In the summer or in hot weather, it’s recommended that people stay in the shade or indoors in the afternoon. It’s a great idea to go back to the Cultural Center or resort – perhaps to use this time to take a dot painting workshop, or simply enjoy swimming in one of the resort’s many pools. Finally finish the day by driving to the Uluru Car Sunset Viewing in time to enjoy the beautiful sunset views.
