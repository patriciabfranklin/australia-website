EUREKA SKYDECK AND MELBOURNE AQUARIUM

The Eureka Skydeck, located on the 88th floor of the Eureka Tower, is the highest viewing platform in the entire southern hemisphere and offers great views of the entire city and beyond. It’s a great way to get a visual recap of the places in Melbourne you’ve already visited and see all the sights from an entirely different angle. Once back down at ground level, head to Melbourne Aquarium to discover the amazing range of creatures from a variety of seascapes including the depths of Antarctica, Rivers, Reefs and the weird and wonderful that live further down below the ocean’s surface. Melbourne Aquarium is in easy walking distance of Eureka Skydeck so you won’t need to take public transport!
