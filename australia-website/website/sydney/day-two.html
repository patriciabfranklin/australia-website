SYDNEY HARBOR CRUISE

Sydney Harbor looks wonderful from the land, but it’s even more impressive when viewed from the water. Board a Sydney Harbor sightseeing cruise and soak in some impressive views of the city’s icons and landmarks including the Sydney Opera House and Harbor Bridge, all of which combine to make Sydney Harbor one of the most famous and beautiful in the world. There are a host of different cruise itineraries available – each of differing prices, vessel type and cruise options.
