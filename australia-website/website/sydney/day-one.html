SYDNEY HOP ON HOP OFF TOUR & FERRY PASS

Offering an independent and easy way to get around Sydney and view its best attractions, this hop on/hop off tour aboard a double decker bus allows you to discover vibrant Sydney at your own pace. Stay on board for a 90-minute guided tour of the city or simply hop on and hop off at various sites all day â€“ the choice is up to you! All the major stops of Sydney are covered here, including the likes of Bondi, Manly, the Rocks, Circular Quay and more, meaning that no essential sightseeing location will go unmissed. Meanwhile the Ferry pass allows you to take advantage of the excellent transit system around Sydney Harbor to explore its stops as well while taking in the sights of all the landmarks from the water.
